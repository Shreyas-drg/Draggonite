About us

Global Competitive Spirit is the trademark of all the technical institutes of the world, as globalization has indeed arrived on the wings of Science and Technology and is here to stay. CSIT, is the Best Engineering College in Chhattisgarh, an AICTE recognized ISO 9001-2015 Certified Institute. It is affiliated to Chhattisgarh Swami Vivekanand Technical University, Bhilai and is a premier Center for Excellence, having laid its foundation in the year 1999.

Our Vision

To nurture world-class intellectual growth by imparting high-quality, futuristic technical education to emerge as one of the best technical institutes to serve mankind.

Our Mision

  • Creating an inspiring environment that promotes innovations, research, and creativity.
  • Providing quality technical education with global standards.
  • Accomplishing a National Centre of Excellence in collaboration with industry.
  • Establishing international alliances and collaborations with foreign universities to achieve global excellence.
